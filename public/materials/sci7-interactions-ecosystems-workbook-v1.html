Energy flow

In the food web: willow → beaver → wolf → decomposers

a) What do the arrows in a food chain represent? (What travels in the direction of the arrow?)

b) Wolves were eliminated from Alberta's national parks during much of the 20th century. Elk populations grew dramatically. Willows and aspens along riverbanks were heavily grazed down. Beavers declined because their food source declined. Riverbanks eroded without willow roots.

Trace this chain of effects. Explain why removing one species (wolves) caused changes three and four steps removed from it.

c) What does this sequence tell us about why decomposers are essential — and not just "optional cleanup crew" — in the nutrient cycle?
