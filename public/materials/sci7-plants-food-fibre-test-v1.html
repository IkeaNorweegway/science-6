20. Read the scenario and answer the questions. (7 marks)

Jan Baptist van Helmont planted a willow seedling in a pot of dry soil weighing 90 kg. He watered it regularly but added nothing else. After 5 years, the willow weighed 74 kg more than when he planted it. The soil weighed only 57 grams less.

a) What does this experiment show about where plants get their mass? (2 marks)

b) The soil lost only 57 g over 5 years. What did the soil provide to the plant? (1 mark)

c) Where did the 74 kg of new plant mass actually come from? Name the specific inputs. (2 marks)

d) What does this tell you about the old belief that plants "eat soil"? (2 marks)
