Using your knowledge of structural types, loads, internal forces, stability, and materials from this unit, write a 5–8 sentence analysis of this failure. Your response should:

  • Classify what type of structure the Tacoma Narrows Bridge was
  • Identify which type of load caused the failure
  • Explain what kind of internal force was likely responsible for tearing the bridge deck apart
  • Describe one design change that modern suspension bridges use to prevent this (hint: open-mesh roadway decks and stiffening trusses)
