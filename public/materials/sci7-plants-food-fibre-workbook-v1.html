Photosynthesis

b) Van Helmont did not know about photosynthesis. If you were advising him, what follow-up experiment could he do to test whether air is involved in plant growth? Describe it briefly.

Plant reproduction

a) Is this grove reproduced sexually or asexually? Name the specific type of vegetative reproduction occurring.
