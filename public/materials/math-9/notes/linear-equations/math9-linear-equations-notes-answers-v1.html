Unit Synthesis

Part A — Key ideas

QuestionAnswer
1. Steps for multi-step equation(1) Distribute brackets. (2) Collect like terms each side. (3) Move variable terms to one side. (4) Move constants to other side. (5) Divide to isolate. (6) Verify.
2. When to flip inequality signWhen multiplying or dividing both sides by a negative number — negative multiplication reverses the order of the number line.
3. Open vs. closed circleOpen: endpoint NOT included (< or >). Closed: endpoint IS included (≤ or ≥).
4. Verifying equation vs. inequalityEquation: substitute and confirm LHS=RHS. Inequality: choose a value from the solution set, confirm it satisfies; choose one outside the set, confirm it doesn't.

Part C — Pre-Knowledge Check reflection

  • 3x+5=14: 3x=9 → x=3. Verify: 9+5=14 ✓
  • Inequality changes: same steps BUT sign flips when dividing by negative; solution is a range, not one value.
  • Special cases: one solution (2x=6 → x=3); no solution (2x+3=2x+7 → 3=7, contradiction); infinitely many (2x+4=2(x+2) → 4=4, always true).
