Part C — Pre-Knowledge Check Revisited

  • Similar ≠ congruent. Similar = same shape, possibly different size. Congruent = same shape AND same size.
  • Equal in similar triangles: all three angles (corresponding angles). Proportional: all three sides (same scale factor).
