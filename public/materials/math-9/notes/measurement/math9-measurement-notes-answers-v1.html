WE — Cylinder (r=4, h=10) on prism (20×20×5):

PartWork
V of prism20×20×5 = 2000 cm3
V of cylinderπ(16)(10) = 160π
Total V2000+160π ≈ 2502.65 cm3
Hidden area (circle)π(16) = 16π cm2
Prism SA2(400)+4(100) = 1200 cm2
After subtracting circle from prism1200−16π
Cylinder SA32π+80π = 112π
After subtracting bottom circle112π−16π = 96π
Total SA(1200−16π)+96π = 1200+80π ≈ 1451.33 cm2

We subtract hidden circle from BOTH shapes because each shape's full SA would include that circle — but in the composite, it's interior to both.

Common mistake: Adding both full SAs double-counts the two touching faces. Must subtract 2 × (hidden face area).
