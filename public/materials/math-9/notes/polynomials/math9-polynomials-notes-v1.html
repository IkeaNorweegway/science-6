To combine like terms: add or subtract the only. The variable part stays the same.

Example
3x2 + 7x2 = x2
The x² part does not change. Only the coefficients are added.
Misconception

"3x² + 2x = 5x²"  or  "3x² + 2x = 5x³"

3x² and 2x have different powers. They represent different things — you cannot combine them any more than you can add 3 apples and 2 oranges and call the result 5 apples.

✓ 3x² + 2x stays as 3x² + 2x — it is already fully simplified.

Misconception

"x × x = 2x"

x + x = 2x (addition doubles the coefficient). But x × x means "x groups of x" — that is x², the area of a square with side x. Addition and multiplication are different operations.

✓ x × x = x²   |   x + x = 2x
