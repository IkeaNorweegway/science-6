2. Identify like terms and combine

(a) 3x² + 5x − 2x² + 4 − x:

Like term groups: {3x², −2x²} and {5x, −x} and {4}

3x²−2x²=x² | 5x−x=4x | constant=4

Simplified: x²+4x+4

(b) 6x² − 3x + 4x² + 2x − 9 + x²:

x² terms: 6x²+4x²+x² = 11x² | x terms: −3x+2x = −x | Constants: −9

Simplified: 11x²−x−9

3. Add polynomials

(a) (2x² + 3x − 1) + (x² − 5x + 4):

x² terms: 2x²+x² = 3x² | x terms: 3x+(−5x) = −2x | Constants: −1+4 = 3

Sum: 3x²−2x+3

(b) (x² + 2x) + (3x − 4) + (−x² + 1):

x²: x²+(−x²) = 0. x: 2x+3x=5x. Constants: −4+1=−3. Sum: 5x−3

4. Subtract — distribute the negative first

(a) (5x² + 2x − 3) − (2x² − x + 1):

Distribute: 5x²+2x−3 − 2x² + x − 1
Simplified: 3x²+3x−4

Common Mistake

−(−x) = +x. Don't keep it as −x.

(b) (3x + 7) − (5x − 2):

Distribute: 3x+7−5x+2
Simplified: −2x+9

(c) Subtract (x²−4x+2) from (3x²+x−5):

"From B" means B − A: (3x²+x−5)−(x²−4x+2)

= 3x²+x−5−x²+4x−2

= 2x²+5x−7

5. Monomial × polynomial

(a) 4x(3x² − 2x + 1):

= 4x(3x²) + 4x(−2x) + 4x(1) = 12x³ − 8x² + 4x

(b) −3x(x² + 4x − 5):

= −3x³ − 12x² + 15x

(c) 2x²(x − 3):

= 2x³ − 6x²

7. Error correction

(a) −(3x²−2x+1) = −3x²−2x+1:

Error: sign of −2x not flipped. −(−2x) = +2x.
Corrected: −3x²+2x−1

(b) 5x × 2x = 10x:

Error: only doubled the coefficient — didn't multiply the variables (x×x = x²).
Corrected: 10x²

(c) (x+4)² = x²+16:

Error: missed the middle term. (x+4)(x+4) has four partial products.
Corrected: x²+8x+16

8. Alberta context — canola field

(a) Perimeter:

2(2x+3) + 2(x²+4x−1)

= 4x+6 + 2x²+8x−2

= 2x²+12x+4

(b) Degree of area (2x+3)(x²+4x−1):

Degree: 3 — the highest-degree terms multiply as (2x)(x²) = 2x³. Degree of product = sum of degrees of factors.

9. Expand and simplify

(a) 3x(x+2) + x(x−4):

= 3x²+6x + x²−4x = 4x²+2x

(b) 2(x+1)(x+3):

(x+1)(x+3) = x²+4x+3 → × 2: 2x²+8x+6

(c) (x+2)² − (x−1)²:

(x+2)² = x²+4x+4 | (x−1)² = x²−2x+1

Difference: (x²+4x+4)−(x²−2x+1) = 6x+3

10. Rectangular garden with square cut

(a) Area of garden (before cut):

(x+7)(x−2) = x²+5x−14 → x²+5x−14 m²

(b) Area of square cut:

x² m²

(c) Remaining area:

(x²+5x−14) − x² = 5x−14 m²

Note: The remaining area is linear — once you subtract x² from x², only the boundary terms remain.
