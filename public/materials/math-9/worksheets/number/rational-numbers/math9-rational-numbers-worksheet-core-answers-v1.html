Rational Numbers

Core Practice ★ — Answer Key

Teacher Answer Key — Not for Distribution

Cover the answer. Try the question. Then check.

1. Order: −2/3, 1/4, −1/2, 0, 3/4

Convert to decimals: −2/3 ≈ −0.667 | −1/2 = −0.5 | 0 | 1/4 = 0.25 | 3/4 = 0.75

Least → greatest: −2/3, −1/2, 0, 1/4, 3/4

Number line: −2/3 is just past −0.5 to the left; −1/2 at midpoint; 0 at centre; 1/4 and 3/4 to the right.

2. Adding and subtracting

(a) 3/4 + 1/6

LCD of 4 and 6 = 12
3/4 = 9/12 · 1/6 = 2/12
9/12 + 2/12 = 11/12

(b) −5/8 − (−1/4)

Rewrite: −5/8 + 1/4
LCD = 8 · 1/4 = 2/8
−5/8 + 2/8 = −3/8

Common Mistake

Forgetting to flip the sign of the subtracted term. −5/8 − (−1/4) is NOT −5/8 − 1/4.

(c) 1/3 + (−3/4)

LCD = 12 · 1/3 = 4/12 · −3/4 = −9/12
4/12 + (−9/12) = −5/12

3. Multiplying

(a) (−2/3) × (3/5)

Sign: neg × pos = negative
(2 × 3)/(3 × 5) = 6/15 = −2/5

(b) (−7/10) × (−5/14) — cross-cancel

Sign: neg × neg = positive
Cancel 7 ↔ 14 (÷7): 1 and 2 · Cancel 5 ↔ 10 (÷5): 1 and 2
(1/2) × (1/2) = 1/4

(c) (−1/4)2

= (−1/4) × (−1/4)
neg × neg = positive
= 1/16

Common Mistake

(−1/4)2 ≠ −1/16. The negative base squared always gives a positive result.

4. Dividing

(a) (3/4) ÷ (1/2)

= (3/4) × (2/1)
= 6/4 = 3/2

(b) (−5/6) ÷ (5/3)

= (−5/6) × (3/5)
Cancel 5 ↔ 5 and 3 ↔ 6 (÷3): (−1/2) × (1/1)
= −1/2

(c) (−3) ÷ (−3/4)

= (−3/1) × (−4/3)
neg × neg = positive
= 12/3 = 4

Common Mistake

Forgetting to flip the divisor. (−3) ÷ (−3/4) ≠ (−3) × (−3/4).

5. Alberta context — Banff temperatures

Starting: −5.5°C | Day 1: +4.5 | Day 2: −6.25 | Day 3: +2.75 | Day 4: −3

(a) Total change:

4.5 + (−6.25) + 2.75 + (−3)
= 4.5 − 6.25 + 2.75 − 3
= 7.25 − 9.25 = −2°C

(b) Final temperature:

−5.5 + (−2) = −7.5°C

6. Order of operations

(a) 2/3 × (−3/4) + 1/2

Step 1 (×): 2/3 × (−3/4) = −6/12 = −1/2
Step 2 (+): −1/2 + 1/2 = 0

(b) (1/2 − 3/4)2 ÷ (1/4)

Bracket first: 1/2 − 3/4 = 2/4 − 3/4 = −1/4
Exponent: (−1/4)2 = 1/16
Divide: (1/16) ÷ (1/4) = (1/16) × 4 = 4/16 = 1/4

7. Error analysis

(a) "5/6 − 1/4 = (5−1)/(6−4) = 4/2 = 2"

Error: Subtracted numerators and denominators separately — not a valid operation. Denominators must be equalized first.
Correct: LCD = 12 · 5/6 = 10/12 · 1/4 = 3/12
10/12 − 3/12 = 7/12

(b) "2/3 + 3/4 = 5/7"

Error: Added numerators and denominators separately without finding LCD.
Correct: LCD = 12 · 2/3 = 8/12 · 3/4 = 9/12
8/12 + 9/12 = 17/12

8. Pipe shortfall

(−3/8) × 2½ = (−3/8) × (5/2)
= −15/16
Total shortfall = −15/16 m³

(Negative indicates below target. The magnitude 15/16 m³ is the shortfall.)

9. Exponents

(a) (−1/2)3

= (−1/2) × (−1/2) × (−1/2)
= (1/4) × (−1/2)
= −1/8
(odd power of a negative → negative)

(b) (3/4)2 − (−1/2)2

= 9/16 − 1/4
= 9/16 − 4/16
= 5/16

(c) −(2/3)2

= −(4/9)
= −4/9
(exponent applies to 2/3 only; the leading negative is NOT inside the square)

10. Recipe batches

(a) 5⅝ ÷ 3/4

5⅝ = 45/8
(45/8) × (4/3) = 180/24 = 7.5
Full batches = 7

(b) Fraction of a batch with what's left

Used: 7 × 3/4 = 21/4 = 42/8
Remaining: 45/8 − 42/8 = 3/8 cup
As a fraction of a batch: (3/8) ÷ (3/4) = (3/8) × (4/3) = 12/24 = 1/2 of a batch
