1. Are the rectangles similar?

(a) A: 4×6, B: 6×9:
Ratio of lengths: 6/4 = 1.5 | Ratio of widths: 9/6 = 1.5
Equal? Yes | Similar — scale factor 3/2
(b) A: 3×8, B: 6×15:
Ratio of lengths: 6/3 = 2 | Ratio of widths: 15/8 = 1.875
Equal? No | NOT similar
Common mistake: Checking only one pair of sides. Both dimension ratios must be equal.

2. △ABC ~ △DEF — find missing measurements

AB=8, BC=12, AC=16, angle B=55°; DE=6

(a) Scale factor: 6/8 = 3/4
(b) EF: 12 × 3/4 = 9
(c) DF: 16 × 3/4 = 12
(d) Angle E: 55° (corresponding to angle B)

3. Triangle similarity criteria

(a) △PQR (P=50°, Q=70°) and △XYZ (X=50°, Z=60°):
Angle R = 180−50−70 = 60° | Angle Y = 180−50−60 = 70°
Matching: P=X=50° ✓ and R=Z=60° ✓
Criterion: AA | △PQR ~ △XYZ
(b) △ABC (4,6,8) and △DEF (6,9,12):
DE/AB=6/4=3/2 | EF/BC=9/6=3/2 | FD/CA=12/8=3/2
All equal? Yes | Criterion: SSS | △ABC ~ △DEF

4. DE ∥ BC, AD=4, DB=6, DE=3

(a) Why △ADE ~ △ABC:
Angle A is shared (common angle). Since DE∥BC, angle ADE=angle ABC and angle AED=angle ACB (corresponding angles). AA similarity criterion.

(b) Scale factor: AD/AB = 4/(4+6) = 4/10 = 2/5
(c) BC: DE/BC = 2/5 → 3/BC = 2/5 → BC = 7.5

5. Solve the proportion

(a) x/6 = 4/8 = 1/2: x = 3
(b) 5/x = 15/9: 15x = 45 → x = 3
(c) (x+2)/4 = 6/3 = 2: x+2 = 8 → x = 6

6. Scale 1:250,000

(a) 3.2 cm on map:
3.2 × 250,000 = 800,000 cm = 8 km

(b) Trail 8.5 km real:
8.5 km = 850,000 cm. Map = 850,000/250,000 = 3.4 cm

(c) Lake 2.4 cm × 1.8 cm:
2.4 × 250,000 = 600,000 cm = 6 km × 1.8 × 250,000 = 4.5 km

7. Shadow method — building, Edmonton

(a) Proportion: Building height / 36 = 2 / 1.2
(b) Building height: h = 36 × (2/1.2) = 36 × (5/3) = 60 m
(c) Why same time? The sun's angle changes throughout the day — measuring at different times creates different-sized angles, so the triangles are no longer similar and the proportion doesn't hold.

8. Scale factor 3/2, △PQR (PQ=8, QR=10, PR=12)

(a) △STU sides:
ST = 8 × 3/2 = 12 cm | TU = 10 × 3/2 = 15 cm | SU = 12 × 3/2 = 18 cm
(b) Perimeter △PQR: 8+10+12 = 30 cm
(c) Perimeter △STU: 12+15+18 = 45 cm
(d) Ratio: 45/30 = 3/2 = scale factor ✓

9. △ABC ~ △DEF, AB=2x+1, DE=3x-1, BC=5, EF=10

(a) Set up proportion: (2x+1)/(3x-1) = 5/10 = 1/2
(b) Cross-multiply: 2(2x+1) = 1(3x-1) → 4x+2 = 3x-1 → x = −3

⚠️ Note for teacher: x=−3 produces negative side lengths (AB=−5, DE=−10), which are geometrically invalid. The expressions chosen in this question are inconsistent — there is no positive solution. This appears to be a typographical error in the worksheet. The question is valuable for practising proportion setup and cross-multiplication, but the expressions should be checked before assigning.

10. Mirror method — grain elevator

(a) Proportion: Elevator height / 72 = 1.65 / 3
(b) Elevator height: h = 72 × (1.65/3) = 72 × 0.55 = 39.6 m
